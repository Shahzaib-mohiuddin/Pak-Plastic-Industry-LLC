WHO WE ARE

Where technology and innovation meet reusable solutions

Nexgen Plastic Industries is part of a manufacturing group with over 30 years of global experience in the plastic manufacturing sector, built on a strong foundation established through multiple production facilities across Asia. Leveraging this extensive industry expertise, we have strategically expanded into the UAE to provide advanced injection moulding and rigid packaging solutions for regional and international markets.

WHAT WE DO

Reusable load carriers for efficient and sustainable supply chains

  • We provide businesses with durable, reusable plastic pallets, crates, and waste bins that cut waste, lower costs, and enable circular logistics solutions.
  • Long‑lasting products engineered for high reuse across warehouses and logistics
  • Washable, safe, and standardized for clean, efficient handling and automation
  • Lower replacement costs and reduced damage vs. single‑use packaging
  • Supports a closed‑loop economy through recyclability and recovery

Sustainable impact at scale

Find us in the UAE and across the Middle East to support smarter circular supply chains.
