Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our logistics and warehousing packaging solutions.

What types of logistics packaging do you offer?

We offer stackable containers, foldable crates, pallet containers, and custom solutions designed for distribution centers, warehouses, and supply chain operations.

Are your containers compatible with automated systems?

Yes. Our containers are designed with consistent footprints and robust construction for seamless integration with conveyors, robotics, and automated storage systems.

How do foldable containers save costs?

Foldable containers collapse flat when empty, reducing return transport costs by up to 75% and saving valuable warehouse storage space.

How durable are your logistics containers?

Our containers are built for long-term use, providing reliable performance for many years of repeated handling and transport cycles.

Are your products recyclable?

Yes. All our packaging is designed to be 100% recyclable at the end of its useful life, supporting sustainable logistics operations.
