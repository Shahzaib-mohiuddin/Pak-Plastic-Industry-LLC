FAQ

We understand that choosing the right packaging can raise questions. Our packaging solutions are built to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and support sustainability.

What types of packaging do you offer?

We offer a complete portfolio of primary and returnable transport packaging for industries including logistics, automotive, food, industrial and chemicals and many more. Our packaging enables efficiency, protection and sustainability throughout the supply chain.

How does reusable packaging benefit my business?

Our returnable packaging reduces costs, simplifies operations, and keeps materials in circulation. It supports efficiency and sustainability, helping your business work smarter while lowering environmental impact.

Are your packaging solutions suitable for automated warehouses?

Yes. Our packaging is specifically designed for smooth handling in automated warehouses and distribution centres. Consistent footprints and robust designs ensure seamless compatibility with conveyors, robotics and automated storage systems.

How do your packaging solutions improve supply chain efficiency?

Stackable, nestable, and foldable designs reduce transport and storage space. Ergonomically designed containers simplify handling and cleaning. Both primary and returnable packaging help reduce damage, lower replacement costs, and streamline operations.

How durable are your returnable packaging solutions?

Our returnable packaging is built for long-term use, providing reliable performance for up to 15 years. Designed for repeated handling and transport, it delivers consistent protection, reduces replacement costs, and supports efficiency across your supply chain.

Are your packaging products recyclable at the end of their life?

Yes. All our new packaging is designed to be 100% recyclable.

Can your packaging solutions be customised?

Yes. Our packaging solutions can be tailored in size, shape, colour, branding, and labels to meet your business needs. Sustainable material options are available to support environmental goals while ensuring product protection and operational efficiency.

Do you offer trade moulding solutions?

Yes. We manufacture a broad range of products, from plastic bottles to pallet boxes, delivering efficient and cost-effective trade moulding solutions. Our capabilities support sectors such as construction, education, and automotive.
