FAQ

We understand that choosing the right packaging can raise questions. Our packaging solutions are built to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and support sustainability.

What types of packaging do you offer for pharmaceuticals?

We offer a complete portfolio of primary and returnable transport packaging for the pharmaceutical industry including IBCs, pallets, pails, and containers. Our packaging enables efficiency, protection and sustainability throughout the supply chain.

What types of primary packaging do you offer?

Our primary packaging includes thin-wall packaging, pails, overcaps and bottles for pharmaceutical, petcare, industrial and homecare products. Containers are made from durable, recyclable polypropylene or polyethylene, protecting products and supporting regulatory compliance.

How does reusable packaging benefit my pharmaceutical business?

Our returnable packaging reduces costs, simplifies operations, and keeps materials in circulation. It supports efficiency and sustainability, helping your business work smarter while lowering environmental impact.

Do your packaging solutions meet pharmaceutical standards?

Yes. Our packaging solutions are made from clean, durable materials that meet strict pharmaceutical standards. They are produced in ISO-certified facilities and ensure full compliance with industry regulations.

How durable are your returnable packaging solutions?

Our returnable packaging is built for long-term use, providing reliable performance for up to 15 years. Designed for repeated handling and transport, it delivers consistent protection, reduces replacement costs, and supports efficiency across your supply chain.

Are your packaging products recyclable at the end of their life?

Yes. All our new packaging is designed to be 100% recyclable.

Can your packaging solutions be customised?

Yes. Our packaging solutions can be tailored in size, shape, colour, branding, and labels to meet your business needs. Sustainable material options are available to support environmental goals while ensuring product protection and operational efficiency.

Do you offer trade moulding solutions?

Yes. We manufacture a broad range of products, from plastic bottles to pallet boxes, delivering efficient and cost-effective trade moulding solutions. Our capabilities ensure precise, high-quality production at scale while meeting each client's specific requirements.
