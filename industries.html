Focus Industries

Pak Plastic Industry delivers durable, high quality packaging and custom engineered products for many industries. We offer reusable transport packaging, bulk systems, rigid food packaging, pails, thin wall containers and tailored trade moulded components.

Our solutions are built to perform in complex environments and produced with advanced moulding technologies. With integrated Design and Engineering teams and in house Materials Innovation, we turn customer challenges into reliable and sustainable packaging that improves logistics, reduces waste and supports circular operations.
