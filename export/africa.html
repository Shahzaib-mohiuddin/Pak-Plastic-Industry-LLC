Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that exporting to Africa requires specialized knowledge. Find answers to common questions about our Africa export operations.

Which African countries do you serve?

We serve all major African markets including South Africa, Nigeria, Kenya, Egypt, Morocco, Ghana, Tanzania, Uganda, Ethiopia, and other African countries. Our distribution network covers the entire continent.

What products are suitable for African markets?

We offer a complete range of plastic pallets, crates, and bulk containers specifically designed for African conditions. Our products are optimized for mining, agriculture, manufacturing, and logistics applications across the continent.

Do you provide customs clearance support?

Yes, we provide comprehensive export support including customs documentation, compliance with African regulations, and coordination with local partners to ensure smooth delivery to any African destination.

What is your minimum order quantity for Africa exports?

Minimum order quantities vary by product type and destination. We offer flexible solutions for both large industrial projects and smaller business needs across African markets. Contact us for specific MOQ information.

How long does delivery to African countries take?

Delivery times vary by destination but typically range from 2-6 weeks depending on the specific African country, order size, and shipping method. We provide tracking and regular updates throughout the delivery process.
