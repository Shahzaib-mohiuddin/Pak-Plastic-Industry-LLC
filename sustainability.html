Your questions, answered

We understand that switching to returnable packaging can bring up many questions. Whether you're looking to improve efficiency, reduce costs, or make your business more sustainable, we're here to help.

How does reusable packaging benefit my business?

Save money, cut waste, and boost efficiency. Our reusable packaging lowers costs over time, streamlines operations, and supports sustainability by reducing the need for constant new packaging.

What returnable transport solutions do you offer?

We offer reusable crates, pallets, and containers designed to meet the needs of various industries, including Automotive, Food & Food processing, Beverage, Retail, Chemicals, Pharma & Cosmetics, and Industrial Manufacturing.

How durable is your reusable packaging?

Built to last. Our reusable packaging is designed for years of use—up to 15 years—delivering long-term value and reliability in your supply chain.

How does reusable plastic contribute to sustainability?

Less waste, lower carbon footprint, smarter resource use. By reusing packaging, we keep materials in circulation, reducing the need for new raw materials and supporting a circular economy.

Can your plastic products be fully recycled?

Yes, our products are recyclable at the end of their use. Plus, with our take-back programs, we collect, recycle, and give them a new life—closing the loop for a more sustainable future.

Are your products made from recycled materials?

Yes, many of our products are made from post-consumer plastics and other recycled waste—high-performance, durable, and sustainable.
