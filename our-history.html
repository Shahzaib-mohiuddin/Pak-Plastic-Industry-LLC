A LEGACY OF INNOVATION

Building excellence in plastic manufacturing

Pak Plastic Industry has grown from a small manufacturing unit to become one of the leading plastic product manufacturers in the UAE, serving customers across the Middle East and beyond.

OUR JOURNEY

Milestones that shaped our success

2009

The Beginning

Pak Plastic Industry was founded in the UAE with a vision to provide high-quality plastic products to local businesses. Starting with a small manufacturing facility and a dedicated team of 10 employees.

2012

Expansion & Growth

Expanded our manufacturing capabilities with new injection molding machines. Introduced our first line of industrial containers and pallets, serving the logistics and warehousing sector.

2015

Regional Recognition

Achieved ISO 9001 certification for quality management. Expanded our client base across the GCC region, becoming a trusted supplier for major retail and food industry players.

2018

Innovation & Technology

Invested in state-of-the-art manufacturing technology. Launched our sustainable product line using recycled materials, marking our commitment to environmental responsibility.

2021

Sustainability Focus

Established our dedicated sustainability division. Implemented circular economy principles across our operations, reducing waste and increasing use of recycled materials to 40%.

2024

A New Chapter

Today, Pak Plastic Industry employs over 200 people and operates from a modern 50,000 sq ft facility. We continue to innovate and lead the way in sustainable plastic packaging solutions across the Middle East.

Join us as we continue to shape the future of sustainable packaging solutions.
